Maze Hiker
December 13th, 2003

Description
-----------
Maze generator / solver with a 3D raycasting engine.  It's
intended for older (m68k) devices, but should run on newer
ones too.  Maze Hiker is released under the GNU GPL, see
license.txt for details.

Requirements
------------
A device that supports 8-bit color (OS 3.5+).

Development
-----------
Mix of C and Assembly, made with PRC-Tools.
